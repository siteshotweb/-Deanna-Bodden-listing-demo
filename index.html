The property

New construction, lakeside living.

Lot 2 at 100 Watershore Drive is a brand-new, luxury pre-construction bungalow in the award-winning Residences at Watershore community in Stoney Creek (Hamilton), Ontario. Three spacious bedrooms unfold across 1,674 square feet of main-floor living on a premium 49 x 109 ft lot, with a flexible 2-bedroom layout also available.

Lakeside living, built new from the ground up.

The bright, open-concept interior is finished with a chef-inspired kitchen, cozy gas fireplace, and main-floor laundry, while a full unfinished basement with sump pump leaves room to grow. Outside, high-end brick and stone give way to a covered rear porch, an attached double garage, and a private tandem driveway fitting up to four vehicles.
